News Media Analysis

  1. Live video source is delivered (over IP)
  2. AWS Elemental MediaLive transcodes to audio and video into multiple bitrates
  3. AWS Elemental MediaPackage packages the bitrates into HLS
  4. Amazon CloudFront distrbutes content, using the MediaPackage as origin
  5. Amazon Elastic Container Service (ECS) orchestrates a task to transcode and stream audio into Amazon Transcribe
  6. Amazon Transcribe transcribes audio and results are sent to Amazon Kinesis Data Streams
  7. Client plays HLS stream and displays transcription
  8. Video archive and transciption is stored in Amazon S3 bucket
